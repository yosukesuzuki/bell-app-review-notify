AppStore Review Notification for Slack

 

What's this App?

This Slack application sends AppStore review notification to your Slack channel.

You can receive reviews of any AppStore App. So, for example, you can check reviews of your competitors.

This application checks periodically review feeds, and sends notification if there are new reviews.

How to Use

  1. Start settings by clicking "Add to Slack" button.
  2. You are redirect to Slack authorization page, choose your team and channel.
  3. You are back to this application, and configure which iOS app you want to receive notification.
